Bellarmine VEX Tournament - Parent Volunteer Signup

November 20, 2015 - November 21, 2015

Hello parents,

Our annual VEX tournament is coming up, and we would not be able to run the tournament without your help. Please sign up for whenever you are available, all help is appreciated.

Also, if you know anyone who is or might be interested in helping out with judges or referees, please send an email to trevor@team254.com or fill out this form.

Thanks,
Trevor Salom
VEX Competition and Operations Director

Description of roles:

Field Queuing: Directs students to their Queuing Table, and then directs them to the Student Field Technicians.
Inspection: Goes through a checklist or rules with each team to make sure their robot is legal.
Judge's Assistant: Help judges direct students and teams to enter judging room.
Sobrato Monitors: Make sure food doesn't enter the theater and direct spectators and teams.
Team Queuing: Directs students from the entrance of Sobrato to the Field Queuing areas. Prevent people without robots from entering the Queuing area.
Concessions: Sell and serve food.
Pit Admin: Checks in teams & volunteers. Assists team queuing from the pits, and helps other volunteers as necessary.
