Bellarmine VEX Tournament - Student Volunteer Signup

November 20, 2015 - November 21, 2015


Hello everyone,

As a reminder, everyone on the team is required to attend the Bellarmine VEX Tournament. Those who are not on a VEX drive team are required to volunteer, although they should talk to their VEX captains about when they should volunteer.

Also, if you know anyone who is or might be interested in helping out with judges or referees, please send an email to trevor@team254.com or fill out this form.

Thanks,
Trevor Salom
VEX Competition and Operations Director

Description of roles:

Field Reset: Set up the competition field's game elements after each match.
Pit Setup/Teardown: Set up tables and wiring in the Pits on Friday. Help teardown on Saturday.
Field Reset: Set up the competition field's game elements after each match.
Field Technician: Receive teams, place them on the appropriate position on the field, and ensure their robot is connected and ready to compete.
Practice Field Manager: Make sure teams aren't leaving the practice field with game objects, area remains clean, people aren't throwing game objects, and teams take turns using the field.
Skills Field Assistant: Time skills challenges, help reset after each run.
Pit Queuer: Remind teams to queue for their qualification matches.
